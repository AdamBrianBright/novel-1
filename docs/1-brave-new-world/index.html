# 1 Brave new world

Trees. Trees everywhere you look and as far as you can see. The only sound reaching ears are tweeting birds. The crowns are dropping soft shadows and only few rays of light passes through.

— What? What's going on?

A young man asked. He would've shout, but his dry throat kept him quiet.

— Where the hell am I? I surely was… Was… Wait, where was I?

He stood up from the ground just to see an unfamiliar place. Nothing reminded him of concrete jungles he used to be in.

'Alright, Newt, stay calm. Let's see if I'm alone.'

Newt looked around himself briefly. Not a single soul in sight. Not even steps or any traces of anyone, of any creature at all.

'How did I get here? My memories seem a bit blurry. I didn't drink, I never drink, so why? The last thing I remember is reading novels at home. Ok, I'm not getting anywhere with it. For now, let's focus on other things.'

He looked down on himself, turned side to side, observing his body. It didn't hurt, and there are no traces of weariness on his clothes. They felt fresh, like he only dressed a moment ago.

'Those clothes. What a weird looks. I don't remember having them, but at least they suit me.'

“WHAT THE!”

In front of Newt's eyes popped a black box with white text on it. It was floating in midair. Newt tried to touch it, but his fingers went right through, like it didn't exist. A moment later, it disappeared.

— Well, that was unexpected. Either I'm losing my mind or I ended up in one of those isekai things. If it's the last, then when I say things like “system” the panel should appear. No? Status?

— Hmm? I know I'm not the fittest person in the world, but this low? And intelligence… Reminds me of good old RPGs I played. I guess it doesn't change my wits, only increases mana pool and my magic casting ability.

— Strength, vitality, agility, dexterity is understandable. But how does defense work? Is it some sort of shield that covers my body? Or does it increase the strength of my skin?

— Wait, MP? Intelligence? Magic? Does this mean I can use it?

Newt lifted a hand and pointed his palm at the nearest tree.

— Wind blade!
— …
— Maybe I should imagine it in my head?

'Wind blade'

A packed air in the form of crescent blown forth for a meter and faded, leaving a small scratch on a crust.

— It works! I can use magic and without those stupid shouts!

'It took 20 mana for something that weak. Is it because of my skill, or just a normal thing in this world?'

— I see. Well, despite it looking like a game, it is not. So staying alive is my priority. It doesn't tell how much defense will help. Also, this “impacts” could mean that it is not the only thing that counts towards protection.

— Agility and dexterity is helpful, but I can increase them later. Same with strength. Alright. For now, I'll add two points to VIT.

~ HP: 200 / 250

— So increase vitality doesn't increase current health. Oh, right, it “determines the maximum”

— Well, that's enough for now. I need some food, water, and a place to sleep. Off we go!
